Student Help
View grades
 From the homepage, click "Grades." Then, you can view the percentage grade and letter grade for each assignment from each class.

 View notifications
 From the homepage, click "Notifications."

 Chats
 From the homepage, click "Chat rooms."

 File repository
 From the homepage, click "File repository." From here, you can see the files that you have access to. Use the search bar to find your desired file.
From the "Actions" column, you can Download a file or Delete it from your personal repo.
Store your personal files with the "Upload File" button or use "Shared With Me" to view files from other students.

 Calendar
 You can see upcoming assignments directly from the homepage. For full-screen, click "View Full Calendar"
